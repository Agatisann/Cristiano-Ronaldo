CRISTIANO Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, he has played for top clubs such as Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and later returned to Manchester United before moving to Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia in 2023.

Ronaldo is known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, athleticism, and versatility on the field. He has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards (given to the best player in the world), several Champions League titles, and numerous domestic league titles. He’s famous for his powerful shots, heading ability, dribbling skills, and leadership qualities.

Ronaldo has also been the captain of the Portugal national team, leading them to victory in the Euro 2016 and the 2019 UEFA Nations League.

Off the field, he’s built a massive brand with his CR7 line of products, including clothing, footwear, and fragrances, and has a strong presence on social media with millions of followers.
